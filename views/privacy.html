Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: name, email, phone, handle
  • Ride data: posts, requests, participation
  • Verification documents: DL, RC (encrypted)
  • Technical data: IP address, device info

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Connect riders and facilitate rides
  • Verify rider credentials
  • Improve platform features
  • Send important notifications

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We share only:

  • With other users (public profile, ride posts)
  • When required by law
  • With your consent

4. Data Security

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Encrypted sensitive documents
  • HTTPS for all connections
  • Regular security audits

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account
  • Export your data

6. Contact Us

Email: privacy@highwaycruizzers.com
